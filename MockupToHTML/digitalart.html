This is a collection of work that I completed for my Basic Skills in the Digital Arts class at Illinois State University. I am by no means a visual artist, but for some of these assignments I had fun, and the class overall was helpful with getting a better grasp on the creative process which is pretty well universal through all artistic expression. It's important to work consistently and often, even if what you make isn't always good. If you create enough over time, odds are at least something will be worth the effort.
